Gazpacho recipe

Step 1. Prepare the cucumber.

Peel 30 g - 40 g of cucumber and let it soak to reduce its strength so it is not indigestible.

Step 2. In the glass of the food processor, incorporate:

900 g of ripe tomato branch, without the part of the stalk, cut into a few pieces so that it is better crushed by the food processor, but without removing the skin. This is about 5 medium sized tomatoes.

9 g of coarse salt

50 grams of red bell pepper stripped and cleaned (about 1/3 of a medium-sized bell pepper)

20 grams of Italian green bell pepper.

5 g of garlic peeled and without germ

50 g extra virgin olive oil

The cucumber from the previous step, drained and deseeded

Step 3. Process and emulsify

Start beating on the first speed for about 2 or 3 minutes.

When the ingredients begin to be well blended, increase to the second speed.

Add 500 ml of cold water

Lower to first speed and add 50 g of extra virgin olive oil very slowly, adding a trickle of oil.

Let it process for half a minute more and turn it off.

Step 4. Pass through a fine strainer
