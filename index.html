Alberto Ruiz–de–Alarcón

I am Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematics of CUNEF Universidad since January 2025. Previously, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Universidad Complutense de Madrid as part of the Quantum Spain project and, before that, at Eberhard Karls Universität Tübingen within the SFB TRR 352. I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Universidad Complutense de Madrid under the supervision of Prof. David Pérez-García and Dr. András Molnár, supported by an FPI-SO fellowship at ICMAT-CSIC.

My research lies at the interface of mathematical physics and quantum information theory, focusing on emergent phenomena in quantum many-body systems. My interests include the structure and symmetries of tensor network states, the classification of quantum phases of matter, the mathematical formulation of topological order phenomena and anyons, as well as quantum thermal states and the dynamics of open quantum systems.

You can also find me on Google Scholar, ORCiD, WoS and arXiv.
