I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Faculty of Mathematics of the Complutense University of Madrid, under the Quantum Spain project.

Previously, I held a postdoctoral position at the University of Tübingen, Germany, within the DFG's Sonderforschungsbereich TRR 352 project.

I completed my doctoral studies in mathematics at Complutense University of Madrid, under the supervision of Profs. David Pérez-García and András Molnár, supported by a Severo Ochoa FPI contract at the Institute of Mathematical Sciences of the Spanish National Research Council (ICMAT-CSIC).

My interests focus on analytical results for tensor networks and quantum many-body systems, with a particular emphasis on their application in understanding topological order phenomena and classifying quantum phases of matter employing the tools of quantum information and entanglement theory, representation theory and aspects of category and operator algebra theory.

You can also find me on my Google Scholar, ORCiD, arXiv and institutional pages.
