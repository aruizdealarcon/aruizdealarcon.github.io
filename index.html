My Google Scholar, ORCiD, arXiv and institutional personal pages.

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at University of Tübingen, Germany.

I completed my PhD in Mathematics at Universidad Complutense de Madrid, under the supervision of David Pérez-García and András Molnár, supported by a FPI Severo Ochoa contract at ICMAT-CSIC.

My interests lie in tensor networks, topological order and quantum information.
