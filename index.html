My Google Scholar, ORCiD and arXiv pages

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Eberhard Karls University of Tübingen, Germany.

I completed my PhD studies in Mathematics at Universidad Complutense de Madrid, under the supervision of Prof. David Pérez García and Dr. András Molnár, supported by a FPI SO contract at ICMAT-CSIC.

My interests lie mainly in tensor networks, quantum many body systems and quantum information.
