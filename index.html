I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at University of Tübingen, Germany.

I completed my PhD in Mathematics at Universidad Complutense de Madrid under the supervision of David Pérez-García and András Molnár, supported by a FPI Severo Ochoa contract at ICMAT-CSIC. During my studies, I did a research stay at Norbert Schuch's group at the University of Vienna.

My interests lie in the mathematical aspects of tensor networks, topological order and quantum information within the context of quantum many-body systems.
